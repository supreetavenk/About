About Me

Hey! I’m Supreeta Venkatesan, a graduate student at USC’s Viterbi School of Engineering. I did my undergrad in Electronics and Communication Engineering in India, wherein I worked on projects involving microprocessors and Microcontrollers, sensors and many such hardware platforms like FPGA.
